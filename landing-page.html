What's inside

Your quest is structured around the Unleash Brilliance proven methodology and model
LAW 1: Be You: Own Your Spotlight
If you want to have a visible impact as a leader, professional or person then you must start by reconnecting and reclaiming who you are - your strengths and weaknesses; courage and self-doubt.

There are 3 Facets that Law 1 addresses: Character, Focus and Expertise.
LAW 2: Be Ready: Harness Your Energy
Next, you must become masterful at taking back control, thriving energetically and ensuring you are doing all you can to be the best version of you mentally, physically and spiritually.

There are 3 Facets that Law 2 addresses: Mindset, Stamina and Behaviour.
LAW 3: Be Together: Connect with Intent
Only then do you turn your attention outwards because engaging in the right way, surrounding yourself with the right network of people and collaborating effectively to mutual benefit is the only thing that will truly drive change.

There are 3 Facets that Law 3 addresses: Engage, Network and Collaborate.
LAW 4: Be Heard: Magnify Your Influence
Lastly, you shift and amplify your influence so that you lead in your area of expertise and become in demand for what you do. This is how you shine in your brilliance and allow others to shine too.  

There are 3 Facets that Law 4 addresses: Lead, Magnify and Accelerate.
